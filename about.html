You’ve got questions, we’ve got answers.

How long can I expect the fragrance to last on my skin?

The longevity of our fragrances varies based on factors such as skin type, climate, and activity level. However, our carefully crafted scents are designed to have a lasting presence. On average, you can enjoy the captivating notes for 6 to 8 hours. To enhance the longevity, consider applying the fragrance to pulse points and using our complementary scented body products.

Are your perfumes suitable for sensitive skin or allergies?

Our perfumes are crafted with high-quality ingredients and are free from common allergens. However, everyone's skin is unique, and reactions can vary. We recommend performing a patch test before full application. If you have specific sensitivities or allergies, please consult with a dermatologist before use.А

How do you ensure your commitment to sustainability?

We are deeply committed to sustainability and take several steps to ensure our practices are environmentally friendly. Our packaging is made from recycled and recyclable materials, and we minimize waste throughout our production process. Additionally, we source our ingredients ethically and prioritize suppliers who adhere to sustainable practices. We continuously strive to reduce our carbon footprint and support initiatives that promote environmental well-being.
